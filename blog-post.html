5 bookkeeping habits for stress-free tax season

Published August 15, 2025 • 5 min read

Great books start with simple, consistent habits. In this post, we’ll cover five that make year-end a breeze.

1) Reconcile weekly

Frequent reconciliations keep your data accurate and actionable.

2) Separate business and personal

Use dedicated accounts to avoid messy cleanup and missed deductions.

3) Capture receipts

Automate with tools like Expensify or Dext and set clear policies.

4) Review reports

Monthly P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow inform better decisions.

5) Plan for taxes

Quarterly estimates and entity structure can reduce surprises.
